Here are some facts about Gene Kelly!

1. Gene Kelly was a Virgo

2. The final directing credit that Gene Kelly has is co-director of the movie That’s Entertainment, Part 2 (1976)

3. Gene Kelly’s final film as an actor was the movie Xanadu (1980)

4. Gene Kelly’s film debut was in a movie with Judy Garland called “For Me and My Gal” (1942)

5. Gene Kelly shares a birthday with Kobe Bryant and Scott Caan

6. Gene Kelly made a few films with the icon Frank Sinatra ("Anchors Aweigh", "Take Me Out To The Ball Game", and "On The Town")

7. In the movie Anchors Aweigh Gene Kelly danced with Jerry the mouse from the famous Tom and Jerry.

8. Gene Kelly died on the same day as Sid Vicious, and Philip Seymour Hoffman
